    Off the shelf integration has its place and can be fine for simple integrations (although even then there is an ongoing cost), but for more complex integration where real-time synchronisation of two systems is required, bespoke software is the way to go.

    As well as their technical capabilities, Andy & Kat have a wealth of business experience. Andy has been a freelance software developer for over 35 years and has worked in many sectors of industry. Kat has many years’ experience in marketing management in technical industries, before more recently moving into software development. They both have an eye for attention to detail, and a passion for writing clear, concise and grammatically correct documentation.

    Our starting point is always the same; talk to the client to understand how their business currently operates, learn what it is they are trying to achieve, and then propose a solution. What we don’t do is talk in riddles, go on journeys or reach out!

    We are not Zoho Partners – we leave what they do to the experts in that field, but we work closely with them and typically handle the specialised integration aspects of solutions.

    We are UK based (Cornwall & Worcestershire) and have worked on projects across the UK and beyond. We use Zoom, Teams & Google so your geographical location makes no difference to us!

    Are standards are high, but our overheads are low, so you might be pleasantly surprised at how little it costs to streamline your business processes!
